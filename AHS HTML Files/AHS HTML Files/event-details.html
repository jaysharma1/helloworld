Mini Medical School - MMS@KTPH

Come and Join Us at Our Mini Medical School where you can learn in a fun and interactive way on how to care for yourself and loved ones. The 3 Saturday morning sessions are designed to be fun, casual and informative. No background in science required!

About the Event

30 Apr - Programme

Session 1 - What is public health?

Learn the basic principles of public health, epidemiology and disease control

Session 2 - Zika, and other villains in our midst

Understand emerging infectious diseases, travel and health precautions

07 May - Programme

Session 3 - Living in the city, a survivorâ€™s guide!

Know what are pandemics, emerging threats, and outbreak management

Scientific Symposium - Excuse me, did you cause an outbreak?

Interact with experts to understand pandemic preparedness planning, contact tracing procedures, and community responsibility during a public health emergency such as SARS, MERS, Ebola, etc.

14 May - Programme

Session 4 - Raw fish, anyone?

Learn about food safety, lifestyle determinants and public health concerns

Session 5 - Navigating healthcare and public health

Discover the challenges in the healthcare ecosystem, and personal responsibility for health
